STUDENT/FACULTY FEEDBACK SYSTEM

Student Feedback system is used to get the feedback from the students. It generates the reports for the faculty on the basis of given feedback by the students. The staff will be provided with separate usernames and password in order to check the results. The total report is visible to the people like HOD. It contains the modules like student, faculty and admin. Admin is the responsible for creating a class and assigning the corresponding faculty to the class. Within short time we can get the performance of the faculty from the student point of view. There are 8 questions to evaluate the status of faculty and each question contains 5 options like Excellent, very good, good, Average and Poor.
